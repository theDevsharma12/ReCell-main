We are ReCell

We continually strive to push boundaries,
making a meaningful and positive impact on society.

We think beyond

ReCell aspires to facilitate a world powered by the
most environmentally friendly batteries attainable.

We aim to improve by providing superior technologies, a more innovative market approach, and an enhanced business model. We reassess the value chain of lithium-ion batteries, not only for ourselves but also for you and, most importantly, for future generations.

This vision has brought to fruition what was once inconceivable in 2017: recycling 95% of battery materials while simultaneously emitting 75% fewer greenhouse gases than existing solutions.

We're setting ambitious targets. Our aim is for every major city to possess its own ReCell critical mineral extraction facility, supplying ReCell hydrometallurgy plants and furnishing the global market with recycled strategic materials.

And we are committed to reaching that destination! In addition to our state-of-the-art technologies, we depend on a dedicated team to deliver the high-performance solutions demanded by the market.

Our 5 values

  • 01
    Innovation

    The path that led us here won't lead us there

    Excellence isn't our endgame; it's our launchpad. We embrace tough challenges to fuel constant improvement. Through innovation, we refine what works and drive positive transformations. Our strength lies in our relentless pursuit of innovation, continuously seeking betterment.

  • 02
    Perseverance

    Determination gleams in our eyes, smiles grace our faces

    We consistently push beyond our comfort zone, embracing difficult questions and challenges head-on. Laughter is an integral part of our culture at ReCell; it's what keeps us motivated and energized. Balancing work with enjoyment has been key to our success and continues to illuminate our path forward. While we recognize the importance of battery circularity, we maintain a playful spirit and never lose sight of the joy in our journey.

  • 03
    Integrity

    Unyielding in our commitment to circularity

    We aim to establish a circular economy without spinning our wheels. This entails thoroughly assessing the impact of every decision we make. It's a core principle deeply ingrained in our mindset. As perpetual learners and candid communicators, we value clarity and transparency. We pledge to maintain honesty and openness with our customers, colleagues, partners, and stakeholders—without exception.

  • 04
    Collaboration

    Indeed, sharing is a demonstration of care

    Whether partner or colleague, we hold everyone in high regard. Together, we form a cohesive team dedicated to bringing out the best in each other. We understand the importance of unity, setting aside silos and egos to work harmoniously day in and day out. While not always effortless, this approach yields dividends. It enables us to advance collectively, drawing from our shared wisdom and experiences.

  • 05
    Benevolence

    Our ascent is fueled by lifting others

    For us, prioritizing the well-being of all living beings is fundamental. Nurturing the world begins within our own workspace. We cultivate a safe and healthy environment where mutual care is paramount. When making decisions, we value input from everyone, including our partners and teams. Engaging with our communities is a cornerstone of our dedication because, to us, kindness isn't just instinctual; it's indispensable.
