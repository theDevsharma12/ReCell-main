Our Products

Utilizing our exclusive Net Zero Waste and Zero Emissions
methodology, we yield premium-quality products extracted
from Lithium-ion batteries.

Black Mass

The composition of our Black Mass encompasses Lithium, Cobalt, Nickel, Manganese, and Graphite, with impurities accounting for less than 1% of its content.

Lithium (Li)

Derived from the sustainable recycling of Lithium-ion batteries, our superior-grade micronized Lithium Carbonate is meticulously crafted, tailored for a myriad of applications, ranging from battery cells to the production of glass, ceramics, and beyond.

Cobalt (Co)

Sourced from end-of-life Lithium-ion batteries, our premium Cobalt Sulphate is meticulously extracted, perfectly tailored for crafting cathode materials containing Cobalt for both Lithium-ion and Lithium polymer battery cells.

Nickel (Ni)

Derived from spent Lithium-ion batteries, our high-purity Nickel Sulphate is meticulously extracted, ideal for the manufacture of both Lithium-ion and Lithium polymer batteries.

Manganese (Mn)

Harvested from depleted Lithium-ion batteries, our premium Manganese Carbonate boasts superior quality, making it an excellent option for various chemical applications and the production of Lithium-ion battery cells.
